Micro Internship: Open Energy Dashboard

During my participation in the MinT program, I, along with my teammate and mentor, contributed to an open source project called Open Energy Dashboard. This project involves a dashboard that visualizes energy data, allowing users to monitor and analyze energy consumption patterns. Our task was to collect testing data from pre=configured files and use this data to create a test for a section of the program. This experience provided me with valuable insights into the world of open source development and the importance of testing in software projects. It allowed me my first opportunity to work with a team in a professional setting and to contribute to a project that has the potential to make a positive impact on energy management and sustainability.
